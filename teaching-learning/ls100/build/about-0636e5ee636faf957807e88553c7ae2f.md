## About the course

**LS100: Computational Behavioral Sciences** is a 4-credit, research-based course taught at
Harvard University by **Souvik Mandal, Ph.D.** This site is the student-facing companion to the
course lectures, designed to be self-sufficient — guiding you from identifying a research
question to communicating your findings.

All content is open for **non-commercial** use; you are welcome to learn from it or teach with it.

## How to use these materials

- **Learners:** follow the [Start Here](start-here.md) path. Run notebooks locally, in Colab, or in Codespaces.
- **Instructors:** adopt or adapt any module, notebook, or reading guide for non-commercial teaching. Attribution appreciated.
- **Researchers:** the video, audio, and statistics pipelines are designed to be reused on your own behavioral data.
