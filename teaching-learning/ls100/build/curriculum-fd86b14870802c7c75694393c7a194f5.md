This page is for students enrolled in LS100. It maps the modules onto the weekly meeting
schedule. Self-paced learners can follow the same content through [Start Here](start-here.md).

The full curriculum and module description are distributed as documents:

- **Module Description** — a one-page overview of the course.
- **Curriculum** — the topic-by-topic / weekly timeline.

:::{note}
The weekly schedule is finalized each semester. Enrolled students should follow the dates posted
on the course's Canvas site; the modules here are ordered to match that schedule.
:::

## Student expectations

- Work in teams of 2–4, while submitting assignments individually.
- Expected effort: approximately 6–10 hours per week.
- Attend weekly meetings and review assigned materials in advance.
- Present at least one relevant research paper during the semester.
- Participate actively in discussion, peer feedback, and project presentations.
